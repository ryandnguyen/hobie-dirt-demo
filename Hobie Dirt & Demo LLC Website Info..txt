About
------------------------
Hobie Dirt & Demo is a site preparation company specializing in dirt work, demolition, and material hauling for residential, farm and light commercial projects. 

As a small business located in Denton, NE, we serve Lincoln and the surrounding communities and rural customers scattered about. Focusing on customer satisfaction, our goal is to provide excellent service and attention to detail while building long-lasting relationships with our valued customers. We would love to work with you on your next project.
